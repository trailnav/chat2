Support
There are many ways we can offer support to you and your family. We aim to support, rebuild and empower.   SUPPORT: Our fully trained mentors are here to support you through every step of your journey. We offer this online, through the app or email and also offer one to one meetings in certain areas. REBUILD: We can offer help rebuilding your life, offering links to counselling and therapy for you and your family. EMPOWER: Helping you grow in confidence and become the person you want to be by offering advice with education and recruitment for getting back to work or improving your career. Also support groups to meet other people who are going through similar issues.

Message:
What is domestic abuse? There is a wide spectrum to domestic abuse. Any of the list below can be included.  • It can begin with moodiness when the abuser doesn’t get what they want  • Bullying tactics  • Constantly keeping a check on you  • Having to tell them where you are going/who you are with  • Controlling what money you can spend  • Told not to cry/shut up  • Pushing  • Slapping  • Threatening behavior  • Put downs. Your ugly/no one else will want you/ your stupid  • Punching  • Beatings  • Screaming  • Broken bones  • Isolation  • Coercive behavior The list goes on.  Domestic abuse is not only physical abuse, but mental abuse also. If you are experiencing domestic abuse, we are here to offer you support.

Online Support User Agreement Welcome to our online support service. Before we connect you with a mentor, please read this users agreement This is to ensure mentor/mentee autonomy is maintained and you are clear about the service you are using. All conversations are confidential. When having supervision the mentor may discuss your case but your identity will never be revealed. The only time that confidentiality may be broken is if you are at serious risk of causing harm to yourself or others or when we are compelled to do so by law.  Please ensure that you secure your computer and emails against unauthorised viewing by third parties.
